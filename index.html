Allure of the Stars

near-future Sci-Fi roguelike and tactical squad game

Play it in the browser!

===>   http://allureofthestars.com/play

Description

Not a single picture in this game. You have to imagine everything yourself, like with a book (a grown-up book, without pictures). Once you learn to imagine things, though, you can keep exploring and mastering the world and making up stories for a long time. The gameplay manual may also help. Enjoy!

The game is written in Haskell using the LambdaHack roguelike game engine. Long-term goals of the project are high replayability and auto-balancing through procedural content generation and persistent content modification based on player behaviour. Also, making aliens bitterly regret the idea of plotting against Earth.
